Adjectives

Today, we're going to embark on an exciting adventure into the world of adjectives.

Have you ever wondered how we can make our words more colourful, more vivid, and more interesting? Well, that's where adjectives come into play!

Imagine you are in a beautiful garden with many different kinds of flowers. If you just say "flowers", it does not really tell us much about them, does it?

But what if you said "bright red roses", "beautiful white lilies", or "tall sunflowers with yellow petals"? That sounds much better, right?
